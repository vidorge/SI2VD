N.V. Gogolj

Opis

- Atelier 212 by the end of the current theater season, announced the new show. It is about Gogol's comedy "auditor", directed by Iva Milosevic, who talks about the fear corrupt governments in this city since the arrival of auditors, whose premiere is expected on Tuesday, June 4, on the big stage of the Belgrade theater houses. Costumes is done Boris Čakširan, scenery Gorčin Stojanovic, a composer Vladimir Pejković.

Gogol's characters interpreted teams consisting of 19 actors ensemble Atelier 212: Gordan Kičić, Nebojsa Ilic, Branimir Brstina, Gorica Popovic, Fedja Stojanovic, Milan - Caci Mihailovic, Branka Selic, Ivan Jevtović, Mladen Andrejevic, Bojan Žirović, Milica Mihajlovic, Nenad Ciric Radmila Tomovic, Isidora Minic, Branislav Zeremski, Erol Kadic, Vladislav Mihailovic, Jelena Petrović / Sofia Juričan and Dragan Djukic.

- The social comedy in five acts "auditor" engaged in Imperial Russia in the first half of the 19th century. With the hindsight you "read" this Gogol's work?

Gogol in "The Inspector General" with great acuity accounted with those who hold power and who profit from it. He talked about košmarnoj reality of his time showing as a universal orgy pokvarenluka, malice, shamelessness and primitivism, which allows the "brotherhood" of incompetent, irresponsible and incompetent people in management positions, who are in their horrifying mediocrity very aggressive, greedy and ambitious. However, the "auditor" is not a criticism just a bureaucratic layer. It is a curiosity of the dimensions of race and malformations of the chaos that follows. I think the analogy with our reality more than obvious ...
